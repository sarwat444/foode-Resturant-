Foode was opened in November 6, 1959. The authors of the project and name for the then-café were Algimantas and Vytautas Nasvyčiai. The interior of

Foode was created by artists Vladas Jankauskas, Vytautas Povilaitis (mural painting), sculptor Juozas Kėdainis. In November 5, 1970 the interior was listed among State Protected Monuments of Lithuanian Architecture
